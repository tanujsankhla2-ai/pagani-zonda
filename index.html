Common Questions

How is the ice cream shipped?

We ship our ice cream in custom-insulated eco-friendly coolers packed with dry ice. It is guaranteed to arrive at your doorstep frozen solid, no matter where you are in the lower 48 states.

Are your ingredients organic?

We prioritize quality and flavor above all. While a vast majority of our ingredients are organic, we sometimes partner with small heritage farms that practice sustainable agriculture but may not have official organic certification.

Do you offer vegan or dairy-free options?

Yes. Our Velvet Berry Eclipse is a naturally dairy-free sorbet, and we occasionally release limited-batch plant-based alternatives crafted with cashew and coconut cream.

How long does it last in the freezer?

For the optimal creamy texture and flavor experience, we recommend enjoying Botto within 45 days of delivery. However, it will keep safely in airtight conditions for up to 3 months.
